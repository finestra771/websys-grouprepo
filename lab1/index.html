OUR PROJECTS

8th Wonder Website

We designed a website for RPI's Eighth Wonder K-pop and street dancing club. The site contains information about the club and its activities for prospective members, a calender to track club activities, and a form to sign up to the club's waiting list. We made use of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL for the Eighth Wonder website.

Lifeline Website

We developed Lifeline, a web application for tracking assignments and deadlines. The site lets people upload assignments and their deadlines and displays them in an easily visible way. We made use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

ScheduleSync Website

We developed ScheduleSync, a web application which allows students to upload their schedules into ScheduleSync's database and compare their schedules to other students to find out which classes they share. We used MySQL to store the data and PHP to upload it to the database.
